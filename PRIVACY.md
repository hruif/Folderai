# Folderai — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 24 June 2026_

Folderai is built to keep your files private. **Everything happens on your Mac.**

## What we collect
**Nothing.** Folderai does not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your data off
your device. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers operated by us that
receive your files or activity.

## How your files are handled
- **On-device AI.** File classification runs on a local AI model that ships inside the
  app. Your file names and contents are read **only on your Mac**, only to produce the
  organization plan you review. They are never uploaded.
- **Folders you choose.** Folderai only accesses folders you explicitly grant it (for
  example Downloads, Documents, Desktop, or folders you add). You can mark folders as
  **protected** so they are never touched.
- **Nothing is deleted.** Items you choose to remove are moved to a Quarantine folder you
  control and can undo — Folderai never permanently deletes your files.

## Network use
Folderai works fully offline. The on-device model is bundled with the app, so no internet
connection is required for normal use. The app contains no analytics, telemetry, crash
reporting, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Third-party components
Folderai uses the open-source **Llama 3.2** model (© Meta Platforms, Inc.) for on-device
classification. The model runs locally; it does not communicate with Meta or anyone else.
