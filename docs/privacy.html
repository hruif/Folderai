Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026.

Folderai is a local-first macOS app for organizing messy folders with staged review, recoverable cleanup, and on-device AI.

  • No Folderai sign-in, subscription account, or user profile.
  • No event tracking, session tracking, advertising pixels, or usage telemetry.
  • Folder scanning, AI classification, OCR, review, cleanup, quarantine, and undo run locally unless you choose to send email support.

Data Collection

Folderai does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal information through its folder cleanup features. Xintech LLC does not receive your file names, file contents, folder paths, cleanup plans, settings, usage, or device information from the app unless you intentionally send it through email support.

  • No Folderai account is required.
  • No analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
  • No background telemetry is sent to Xintech LLC.

Local Data

Folderai may store local preferences, local cache data, learned organization choices, quarantine manifests, and bounded local diagnostic information on your Mac. This is used to keep the app consistent between launches, speed up repeated scans, recover cleanup actions, and prepare bug-report drafts when you choose to report a bug.

Local data may include:

  • Approved source and destination folders.
  • App settings such as model, cleanup preferences, protected folders, display density, and local learning.
  • Cached AI classifications and learned correction patterns.
  • Quarantine restore manifests for files marked for removal.
  • Bounded local diagnostic messages.

Folderai reads local file metadata and supported file contents only when you choose folders to scan or clean. File content and OCR text are processed locally for classification and search.

Network Activity

Folderai's normal folder scanning, on-device AI, OCR, staged review, cleanup, quarantine, and undo features do not require sending files to Xintech LLC. Updates are expected to be handled through direct downloads from the Folderai website.

Support or feedback items may open a draft in your default email app. The app does not send email automatically, and nothing is sent unless you choose Send.

Bug Reports, Feature Requests, and Feedback

Feedback or bug-report actions may open a draft in your default email app addressed to Xintech LLC. The app does not send email automatically. You can review, edit, or delete the draft before sending it.

The draft may include a short diagnostic summary so support can understand the context, such as app version, macOS version, processor architecture, model status, and current app status. Your email address is visible to Xintech LLC only if you send the email.

Your Choices

  • You can leave feedback drafts unsent, edit them, or delete diagnostic details.
  • You can change or remove approved folders and protected folders in the app.
  • You can clear local cache and learned corrections from the app settings.
  • You can restore quarantined files using the run's restore manifest or app undo flow.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact Xintech LLC at admin@xintechllc.com.

Xintech LLC may update this policy if Folderai's behavior changes. The effective date above will change when the policy changes.
