Folderai — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Folderai is built to keep your files private. Everything happens on your Mac.

What we collect

Nothing. Folderai does not collect, transmit, sell, or store any of your data off your device. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers operated by us that receive your files or activity.

How your files are handled

  • On-device AI. Classification runs on a local AI model bundled in the app. Your file names and contents are read only on your Mac, only to produce the plan you review. They are never uploaded.
  • Folders you choose. Folderai only accesses folders you explicitly grant it (e.g. Downloads, Documents, Desktop). You can mark folders as protected so they are never touched.
  • Nothing is deleted. Items you remove move to a Quarantine folder you control and can undo — Folderai never permanently deletes your files.

Network use

Folderai works fully offline; the on-device model ships with the app. There is no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party tracking.

Third-party components

Folderai uses the open-source Llama 3.2 model (© Meta Platforms, Inc.) for on-device classification. The model runs locally and communicates with no one.
