When Reporting an Issue

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • What happened and what you expected to happen.
  • Your macOS version and Mac model, if known.
  • Whether the issue happened during scan, AI refinement, review, cleanup, or undo.
  • The folder type involved, such as Downloads, Desktop, Documents, or an external drive.
  • The steps that reproduce the issue.

Remove any file paths or details you do not want to send.

Notes

  • FolderAI is designed to stage a cleanup plan before it moves anything.
  • Items marked for removal go to a recoverable quarantine folder.
  • One-click undo is intended to reverse a completed cleanup run.
  • Local AI quality can vary, so review the staged plan before executing.
  • Read the FolderAI Privacy Policy for details.
